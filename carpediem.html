  • I’ve decided to create a book that will shaw a bit of my life that is spread between two countries: Poland and Ireland. I was trying to memorize country of my youth and shaw places and people that are stuck in my head forever.

  • The main subject of that project was word “catching” and through my work I tried to shaw the viewer the moment of catching things, minutes and the joy of living life to the fullest.

  • I’ve called that book “Carpe Diem by Pawel Abram”. Title of this work is the main motto of the final look of this project and is also main motto of my life.

  • I’ve chosen photographs that will shaw important places, close people and passion in my life. The photographs are half from Poland and half from Ireland, which is very important as my roots are still back home but my current life is moved to the new country.

  • Main subject of these photographs is catching- so there are different pictures representing the different types of catching.
